About the Journal

The main task of the journal is to cover the achievements, problems and prospects, history and stages of
development of education and information technology. The journal contains the results of research in the following areas:
05.00.00 - Technical sciences,
13.00.00 - Pedagogical sciences (methods of teaching computer science).

Current Issue
Vol. 1 No. 6 (2022): CAJECS

The main purpose of the journal is to inform the scientific community about the problems of improving research and innovation in this field, to cover the scientific work of experts and scientists in the field through the Internet, to discuss current scientific issues with scientists around the world.
